Lime Ridge is an Open Space Preserve where the protection and rehabilitation of the natural environment is a priority, where families from all over Contra Costa county can hike, bike and picnic.

To improve community availablity, the Walnut Creek Public works department has been working with impact studies on how to to create multiple safe, environmentally conscience Flow trails in Lime Ridge.

 

Flow Trails take mountain bikers on a terrain-induced roller coaster experience, with little pedaling and braking necessary. This style of trail typically contains features like banked turns, rolling terrain, various types of jumps, and consistent and predictable surfaces. Trails are adaptable, and allow to gain the confidence that they need to build out their cycling skills.

 
 

Lime ridge Open Space is a nature preserve. It's amazing collection of geography, access, and trails make it an ideal candidate for bike specific trails.
